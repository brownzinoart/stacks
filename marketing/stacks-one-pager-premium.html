StackSnap Workflow

01

Capture

Point your camera at any bookshelf, whether at a library, bookstore, or friend's collection.

02

Recognize

OCR engine instantly identifies book titles and authors from your photo in real-time.

03

Recommend

AI analyzes identified books and generates personalized suggestions overlaid on your image.

04

Save

Add books to your reading list with a single tap. Organize into custom stacks.

05

Share

Post your interactive shelf photo to your feed, complete with book overlays and recommendations.
